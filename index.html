MEET MR.KASHAN

Hi, I'm Kashan, a Style & Vision Consultant and founder of Specs at Home. My mission is simple: to make buying glasses easier, more affordable, and tailored to your lifestyle. I realised that the traditional eyewear industry hasn't changed for years - customers still face high prices, long waiting times, and the inconvenience of multiple visits to an optician. That's why I created Specs at Home, a mobile optical dispensing service that brings premium-quality eyewear straight to your door.
